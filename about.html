About KindMind

Why we exist

KindMind is a student-led initiative inspired by Active Minds. We exist to create inclusive, trauma-aware spaces where students can discuss mental health without stigma.

Partner With Us

Mission & Vision

Mission: Mobilize youth and young adults to lead a transformative movement in mental health.

Vision: To destigmatize mental health disorders by promoting open, enlightened discussion and creating better lives for all.

How we work

Peer-run events, collaboration with campus services, and student advocacy.

Our Values

Supportive

We prioritize listening and peer care.

Empowering

We equip students with tools and skills.

Inclusive

We welcome diverse experiences and identities.
